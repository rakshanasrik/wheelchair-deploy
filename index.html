Wheelchairs Using Sensors

Done by K. Rakshana Sri

Components Required

  • ATmega8 Microcontroller
  • Voice Control App
  • Bluetooth Module
  • IR Sensors
  • Relays
  • Motors

Methodology

Step-by-step Process:

  • ATmega8 Microcontroller processes voice commands and gestures.
  • Bluetooth connects a mobile app to control movements.
  • Relays manage motor operations based on signals.
  • IR Sensors detect obstacles in the path.
  • Two main parts: transmitter (user's hand) and receiver (chair).
